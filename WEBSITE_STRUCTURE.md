## рҹ”„ User Journey Examples

### Example 1: Finding Waterproofing Products
1. Click "Shop by Products" вҶ’ "Waterproofing & Chemicals"
2. Browse products or use brand filter
3. Click "Request Quote" on desired product
4. WhatsApp opens with pre-filled message

### Example 2: Finding Sika Products
1. Click "Shop by Brands"
2. Search for "Sika" or scroll to Waterproofing brands
3. Click on Sika
4. View all Sika products

### Example 3: Quick Order via WhatsApp
1. Click "WhatsApp" button in header
2. Chat opens directly
3. Send product inquiry
